Freelance Project

Project Overview

Spearheaded the development and implementation of a Solar Tracking System tailored for maximizing energy efficiency in residential settings. The system dynamically adjusts solar panel orientation to follow sunlight throughout the day.

Technical Implementation

  • Used Arduino Uno as the microcontroller
  • Integrated light sensors (photoresistors) to detect sunlight intensity
  • Controlled dual-axis servo motors for panel movement
  • Implemented manual and automatic tracking modes with push button and LED indicators
  • Built with affordable and accessible components: servos, resistors, potentiometers, breadboard

Results & Impact

  • Increased solar panel efficiency by aligning with peak sunlight
  • Reduced power loss due to static positioning
  • Delivered a sustainable and cost-effective solution for Lebanese households facing electricity shortages
